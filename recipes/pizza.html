Odin Recipe

Description

Pizza is a traditional Italian dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Mozzarella
  • Flour
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: Mix 2 ¼ tsp yeast, 1 tsp sugar, and 1 ½ cups warm water; let it froth. Combine 4 cups flour, 1 tsp salt, the yeast mixture, and 2 tbsp olive oil; knead for 10 minutes and let it rise for 1-2 hours.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: Mix 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce, 1 tsp dried oregano, 1 tsp dried basil, and 1 minced garlic clove; set aside.
  3. Assemble the Pizza: Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out the dough, spread the sauce, and add desired toppings (cheese, vegetables, meats).
  4. Bake: Transfer to a pizza stone or baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
