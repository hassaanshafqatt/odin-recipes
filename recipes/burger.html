Odin Recipe

Description

A hamburger, or simply a burger, is a dish consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Dill pickle slices
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Season 1 lb ground beef with salt and pepper; form into 4 patties.
  2. Grill or cook patties on a skillet over medium heat for 4-5 minutes per side, adding cheese slices during the last minute.
  3. Toast burger buns and assemble with lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, and pickles.
  4. Add condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise; place the patties in the buns and serve.
