Odin Recipe

Description

Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil 4 quarts of salted water and cook 1 lb pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat 2 tablespoons olive oil over medium heat; saut√© 3 minced garlic cloves until fragrant.
  3. Add 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, and 1 teaspoon dried basil; simmer for 10-15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta with the sauce; serve with 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves for garnish.
